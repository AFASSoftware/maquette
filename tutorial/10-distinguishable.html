Assignment

The next assignment demonstrates what goes wrong when a non-distinguishable child node is removed and how maquette helps you fix it.

In this assignment, we have two rows in our remote control. When you press the 'remove' button the top row should be removed. Maquette removes the wrong row and then crashes. It is your task is to make the right thing happen. Good luck.
